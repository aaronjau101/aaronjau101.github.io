Summary

The Devil's Eight is a fast paced rhythm-based platformer. Based on Danteâ€™s Inferno, the player must fight its way through the circles of hell with each circle featuring a unique boss. The game has a novel feature that the beats of the music trigger enemy attacks. The player must time the use of a shield to block the attacks of the enemies and reflect the damage back on them.

Contributions

My role in this project was Lead AI Programmer. I was tasked with handling the enemy's animation and collision. I used Unreal's Blueprint Script to create each enemy's Finite State Machine, which handled fluid animations, special attack features, and collision logic. I also used C++ scripts to trigger enemy attacks at certain music beats, spawn platforms, and camera movement. I worked on the bosses Limbo, Lust, and Heresy.
